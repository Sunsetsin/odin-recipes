PB&J Sandwich

Delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich recipe for gods

A delicious peanut butter and jelly recipe for the whole family to enjoy!

INSTRUCTIONS

Step One

Gather all ingredients!

Step Two

Heat a griddle or skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step Three

Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Spread jelly on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread; spread peanut butter on the other unbuttered side.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

    • Steps

    1. Lay out bread
    2. Spread peanut butter
    3. Spread Jelly on left over bread
    4. Lay the bread on top of each other and ENJOY!
