Grilled Cheese Recipe

Yummy Grilled Cheese for the Whole Family

Delicious grilled cheese recipe for the GODS!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • American sliced cheese

    • Steps

    1. Lie out 2 pieces of bread
    2. Place a piece of american sliced cheese on both pieces of bread
    3. Place the bread on top of each other and prepare to put in a skillet
    4. Heat and cook the bread in the skillet until the cheese has melted to satisfaction
    5. Enjoy!
