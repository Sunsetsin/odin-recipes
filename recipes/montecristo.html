Monte Cristo

Beautiful rendition of famous Bennigan's Monte Cristo

Here is the recipe for this perfect mouth watering dish fit for the entire family to enjoy!

Instructions

Sandwich

  1. Assemble sandwiches on a work surface. Start with one slice of bread, three slices of turkey, 1 slice of Swiss cheese, another slice of bread, three slices of ham, 1 slice of cheddar cheese and one final slice of bread.
  2. Slice the sandwich diagonally and then wrap tightly in plastic wrap and chill for 2-3 hours (up to overnight). Chilling allows the sandwich to firm up which will help it hold together in the frying process.
